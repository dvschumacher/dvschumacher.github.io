Introduction

My research interest is machine auditory. With the support of my research lab, my works range from expressiveness analysis to hardware implementation of acoustic signal processing algorithms. The specific research focuses include expressiveness analysis, acoustic signal processing, algorithm simplification and hardware implementation of deep learning algorithms.

I gradudated from Centre for Digital Music (C4DM) in Queen Mary University of London (QMUL) follwing the supervision of Prof. Mark D. Plumbley (FIEEE). I am currently a member of task force on Speech Dialogue and Auditory Processing, China Computer Federation and am also a steering committee member of Conference on Sound and Music Technology and am serving as co-chair of Conference on Sound and Music Technology.
